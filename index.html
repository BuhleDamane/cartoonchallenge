Who Lives in a Pineapple?

SpongeBob SquarePants is the eternally optimistic, yellow sea sponge who lives in a pineapple under the sea in Bikini Bottom.

Since his debut in 1999, SpongeBob has become one of the most beloved and iconic cartoon characters of all time, bringing joy and laughter to millions around the world.

With his infectious laugh, childlike enthusiasm, and unwavering friendship with Patrick Star, SpongeBob teaches us that happiness is a choice and friendship is everything!

üåä SpongeBob's Journey üåä

1999 - The Beginning

SpongeBob SquarePants premiered on Nickelodeon on May 1, 1999. Created by marine biologist Stephen Hillenburg, the show quickly became a cultural phenomenon.

2004 - Big Screen Debut

The SpongeBob SquarePants Movie hit theaters, earning over $140 million worldwide and solidifying SpongeBob as a global icon.

2009 - 10th Anniversary

SpongeBob celebrated a decade of laughter with special episodes and became the most-distributed property of MTV Networks.

2015-Present - Meme Legend

SpongeBob became the king of internet memes, with scenes like "Mocking SpongeBob" and "Imagination" becoming viral sensations across social media.
